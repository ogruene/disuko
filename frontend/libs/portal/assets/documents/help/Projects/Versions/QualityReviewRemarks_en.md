# Review Remarks

## Overview

- Review remarks provide a feedback channel for questions or issues with the disclosure information provided by a software supplier
- Such questions or issues are referred to as "review remarks"
- In addition to single review remarks, an overall review status can be managed

## Lifecycle

- Review remarks can be listed, created, commented, and updated in their status
- Only the creator of a review remark can edit the respective remark, every other user on a project can comment it
- Review remarks are related to components, licenses and SBOM deliveries of a project version and will thus change in their status over time
- After a new SBOM is delivered, which is addressing an issue, the respective review remark may be closed, or adjusted in the problem level
- Review remarks can not be deleted; they can just be put to status closed or cancelled
